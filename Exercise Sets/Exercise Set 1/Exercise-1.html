MPL Philippines Season 14

  1. MPL Philippines

    The Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League Philippines,
    also known as MPL Philippines or MPL-PH, is a premier Esports
    league for the game Mobile Legends: Bang Bang in the Philippines.
    Established in 2018, MPL Philippines has been recognized as the
    strongest Mobile Legends region, producing four world champions
    and winning four consecutive world championship titles by 2023.
